Request Data Removal

You can request that your messages be removed from Tracecord. Requests are reviewed manually and processed within 7 days.

⚠ Before you submit

Please read the following before submitting your request:

  • You must provide your Discord user ID to verify ownership of the account.
  • We can remove your messages from our Tracecord database — we cannot remove messages from Discord itself, or from any other archives.
  • Removal is permanent and cannot be undone once processed.
  • Backfill data and real-time capture will both be removed for your user ID.
step 01
Submit form

Fill out the removal form below with your Discord user ID and reason for removal.

step 02
Review

Your request is reviewed manually within 7 days. We may contact you for verification.

step 03
Deletion

Once approved, all messages, reactions, and behavioral data tied to your user ID are permanently deleted from the database.

common questions
How do I find my Discord user ID? ›
Go to Discord Settings → App Settings → Advanced → Enable Developer Mode. Then right-click your username anywhere and select "Copy User ID".
How long does removal take? ›
Requests are reviewed and processed within 7 calendar days. You'll receive a notification at your email if provided. Complex or ambiguous requests may take longer.
Will my messages be recaptured after removal? ›
After a full removal, your user ID is added to an exclusion list. Future messages will not be captured or stored. Server-specific removals apply only to the server scope you specify.
Can I remove data for someone else? ›
Requests must be submitted by the account owner. We cannot process third-party removal requests without verified account ownership. If you are submitting on behalf of a minor, contact us directly.
